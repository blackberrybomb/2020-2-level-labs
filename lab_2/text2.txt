Not a long time ago there lived an owl named Rena. 
She was pretty smart for an owl, so she studied in university. 
Unfortunately, there was a tremendously difficult discipline that even the smartest owl ever could not master: linear algebra. 
Rena was known to be a brilliant linguist, but she didn't know algebra at all. 
Once her linear algebra teacher, a kind-hearted swan named Svetlana, gave her a difficult assignment to do in a few days, and that's where Rena's adventures began...
